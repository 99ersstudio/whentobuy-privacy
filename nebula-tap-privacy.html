Privacy Policy - Nebula Tap: Space Idle Clicker

Effective date: March 9, 2026

Nebula Tap is a local clicker game. This policy describes what data the app handles and what it does not.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. The app does not transmit user data over the internet in the current release.

2. Local Storage

The app stores game progress and settings on your device (for example upgrade levels, credits, and audio preferences). This data remains on your device.

3. Internet Access

The current release does not require internet connectivity for core gameplay.

4. Third-Party Services

The current release does not use advertising SDKs or analytics for personal data tracking.

5. Children

This app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Deleting Your Data

You can remove saved data by using Reset Progress in the in-app Settings, or by uninstalling the app.

7. Future Versions

If future versions add optional online features (for example online rankings), this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Questions? Contact: 99ers.dev@gmail.com
