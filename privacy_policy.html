Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Overview

"When To Buy Crypto" is a market indicator app that displays publicly available cryptocurrency data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No data shared with third parties by us

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores a small counter on your device (using Android DataStore) to manage ad display frequency. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — Displays occasional interstitial ads. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data according to their own privacy policy. See: Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — Processes in-app purchases. Handled entirely by Google Play. See: Google Privacy Policy

API Calls

The app fetches publicly available market data from third-party APIs (CoinGecko, Binance, Alternative.me, Blockchain.info, CoinMetrics, Mempool.space, Frankfurter). These requests contain your device's IP address, as is standard for any internet request. We do not control how these services handle IP addresses — refer to their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
